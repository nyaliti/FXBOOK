# Chartcraft: The Science and Soul of Smart Trading — Academic Syllabus

## Overview
This syllabus transforms the Table of Contents (TOC) into a comprehensive, academic-style curriculum for "Chartcraft: The Science and Soul of Smart Trading." Designed as a structured trading education program, it builds from absolute beginner foundations to advanced SMC execution. Each module includes detailed learning objectives, practical exercises, end-of-module goals, required tools/resources, estimated duration, and assessment/review criteria.

The syllabus integrates all TOC elements, including cheat sheets, mentor reviews, oral rationales, 10-trade weekly submissions, and assessment rubrics with 70% compliance thresholds. It emphasizes hands-on practice, accountability, and progressive mastery.
