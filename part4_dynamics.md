## 12. Inducements & Liquidity Traps

Markets aren't fair—they're engineered. Inducements lure traders into traps.

Identifying traps: Look for repeated tests of levels without breaks. Traps precede expansions.

Avoiding pitfalls: Use multi-timeframe confirmation; don't chase fakes.

Ranges confine price; killzones amplify volatility.

Range analysis: Measure highs/lows; trade bounces.

Killzone strategies: Enter at session starts (e.g., London open).

## 14. The DRIVE Framework: Direction, Range, Interest, Value, Execution

DRIVE integrates all: Assess direction first, then range/interest, seek value, execute precisely.

Breakdown: Direction (trend), Range (levels), Interest (participants), Value (FVGs), Execution (discipline).

Integration: Apply to every trade.
